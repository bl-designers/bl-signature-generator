How to add your email signature in Gmail

1

Fill in your details on the left and select Copy signature.

2

Open Gmail and select the Settings gear in the top right corner.

3

Select See all settings.

4

Scroll to the Signature section and select Create new.

5

Give your signature a name, for example "Primary signature", then select Create.

6

Click into the signature editor and paste your signature (Ctrl+V on Windows, Cmd+V on Mac).

7

Under Signature defaults, set it as the default for new emails and replies.

8

Scroll to the bottom and select Save changes.
